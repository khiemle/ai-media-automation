# Suno Prompt for Baby Sleep Music

I'd love to help you create sleep music for your baby! Before I write the prompt, a few quick questions would help me tailor it better:

1. **Duration**: How long do you want the track? (5 min, 10 min, 30 min?)
2. **Instrument preference**: Soft piano, ambient synths, nature sounds, lullaby-style, or something else?
3. **Mood/Vibe**: Gentle and calming, dreamy, warm and cozy?
4. **Any specific tempo or feel**: Very slow and meditative, or slightly more soothing but not too slow?

That said, here's a starting prompt if you want to jump in now:

---

## Suno Prompt (Baby Sleep Music)

"Create a gentle, soothing lullaby for babies. Soft ambient soundscape with warm piano and subtle nature sounds (soft rain, gentle wind chimes). Very slow tempo (40-50 BPM), calming melodies that fade gently. No lyrics or minimal humming. Perfect for newborn sleep, peaceful and safe feeling. Duration: 10 minutes of dreamy, comforting music."

---

Feel free to adjust any details based on what you're picturing! Which aspects matter most to you?
